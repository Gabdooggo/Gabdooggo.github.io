Directions

Step 1

You should first preheat the oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees C.

Step 2

Melt sugar in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat until liquefied and golden in color. Carefully pour hot syrup into a deep 9-inch round glass baking dish, turning the dish to evenly coat the bottom then set aside.

Step 3

Crack and whisk ehhs in a lare bowl. Add condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract. Whisk until smooth. Pour the egg mixture on top of caramel in the baking dish. Then place in a deep roasting pan. Carefully pour in enough hot water to come 1 inch up the sides of the dish.
